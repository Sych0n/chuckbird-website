what's in it

more depth than you'd expect
from a bird-themed bot

built around the friend group experience — competitive, chaotic, and genuinely worth grinding.

🐦

bird vs pig factions

choose a side at level 10. birds raid pig castles and duel for dominance. pigs earn passive income and run fortify. switch sides 3 times before you're branded a traitor — with your own exclusive bonus for it.

⚔️

raids + king pig title

100 story levels across 4 chapters, plus infinite Go Mode. live boss fights with HP bars and multiplayer damage. the best duelist on the server wears the King Pig crown and gets +8% on all duels until someone takes it.

🏰

guilds + 48h clan wars

create guilds with skill trees and shared banks. challenge rival guilds to 48h wars with real-time scoreboards. passive war points from raids, duels, and fortify runs — every action counts.

🪶

deep economy

earn feathers from jobs, heists, exploring, and stealing. invest, prestige, and upgrade your nest for passive income. the Black Market opens randomly every few hours — don't miss it.

🥚

eggs + 60 pet species

crack eggs to hatch pets across 6 tiers and 60 species — 30 bird, 30 pig. equip 3 at a time for real passive boosts to feathers, duels, raids, and more. send them on expeditions while you're offline.

🗺️

explore + wild

28 hand-crafted zones. go fishing with a live minigame for rare catches, or hunt pigs for materials. build snares in your nest for idle feather collection while you're away.

🌍

world events

auto-trigger every 30-45 minutes. golden storm doubles all income. pig invasion sends 500 pigs at the whole server. egg rain DMs a random egg to every active member. zero setup required.

🎮

minigames + social

scratch cards, blackjack, roulette, race betting, trivia, coinflip, slots. run heists with your crew. put bounties on rivals. poll your server. ask Chuck the magic 8-ball anything.

🏆

trophies + achievements

47 achievements, 10 collectible trophies, profile badges, a flex leaderboard, and a prestige loop. completionists have a lot to chase — and so do the people who just want to dunk on them.

🚧 work in progress — pixel art, new features, and balance updates dropping regularly.
